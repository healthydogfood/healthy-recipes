Description

Cook amazing healthy recipes for your dog, of any age, on a budget & in your home!

Fresh features your dogs will like -

  • Chef's favorites & Popular Dishes now available
  • Search by ingredients you have in the kitchen. You can specify multiple ingredients by putting commas in between. You can also search by your favorite recipe name.
  • Recipes are curated keeping in mind the ingredients that are nutritious for dogs of any age, small dogs, big dogs, puppy dogs. We try our best here but we would also recommend you to consult your vet if you are new here or are cooking for your dog for the first time.
  • We are small team of two, me and my girlfriend and we try our best to add as many recipes as possible and we would love for you to share your recipes too. Write to us at appseverybody789@gmail.com and we would show your recipe at the top with appropriate credits.
